Step-by-step guide to studying MBBS in your dream destination

✔ Research and Select Your Destination

▼

Choose a country based on education quality, tuition fees, living costs, and language. Popular destinations: USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, Poland, Hungary.

✔ Check Eligibility Requirements

▼
  • Science background (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)
  • Minimum percentage or GPA requirement
  • English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Entrance exams (MCAT, BMAT, NEET)

✔ Prepare Required Documents

▼

Gather academic transcripts, language proficiency proof, SOP, LORs, passport, and medical certificate.

✔ Register & Take Entrance Exams

▼

Register and prepare for MCAT (USA), BMAT (UK), NEET (India), or university-specific exams.

✔ Apply to Universities

▼

Go to the official websites of the universities you are interested in and navigate to the admissions section.Complete the application forms accurately and upload the required documents.

✔ Attend Interviews (if required)

▼

Some universities conduct interviews as part of their admission process. If you are shortlisted, you will be invited to attend an interview.

✔ Recieve Offer Letter

▼

After submitting your applications and attending interviews, you will start receiving offer letters from universities. Carefully review each offer, considering factors such as tuition fees, course structure, and location.

Documents Required

  • ✔ Academic certificates
  • ✔ Proof of language proficiency
  • ✔ Passport-sized photographs
  • ✔ A valid passport
  • ✔ Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • ✔ Letters of Recommendation (LOR)
  • ✔ Medical fitness certificate
